    How We Score and Match VPNs

    Our editorial score combines eight public-data categories (audits, jurisdiction, features, pricing). No VPN pays for placement.

    Our weighted score combines eight categories from verifiable public data — no paid placement in rankings:

    • Speed (20%): editorial score from protocol support and public benchmark reputation
    • Privacy (25%): audit type, jurisdiction, warrant canary, RAM servers
    • Security (15%): encryption, protocols, kill switch, leak protection (provider docs)
    • Streaming (10%): documented platform support and provider reputation
    • Value (10%): monthly price, trials, money-back guarantee
    • Ethics (10%): KAPE framework — ownership, audits, breaches, leadership
    • Apps (5%): Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, router support
    • Connections (5%): simultaneous device limits (unlimited = 100 pts)
    • Our Score (0–100): weighted composite of all eight categories above
    VPN needs vary. A service great for streaming may score lower on privacy. Always check jurisdiction, published audit reports, and your specific use case before subscribing.

    VPN Questions Answered

    Straight answers based on our published methodology and public sources.

    What is the best VPN in 2026?
    ExpressVPN and NordVPN tie at 91/100 in our June 2026 editorial index. ExpressVPN leads on streaming (98/100); NordVPN offers stronger value at $3.39/mo. Surfshark (90/100) is the best pick under $3/mo with unlimited devices.
    Which VPN is the fastest in 2026?
    ExpressVPN has the highest speed score (93/100) in our editorial index, based on WireGuard/Lightway support and public benchmarks—not first-party lab tests. NordVPN scores 89/100 and Surfshark 88/100. On home connections under 100 Mbps, practical differences are small.
    Which VPN is best for Netflix and streaming in 2026?
    ExpressVPN leads our streaming category at 98/100, followed by NordVPN (95/100) and CyberGhost (94/100). Scores reflect documented platform support and provider reputation. Streaming blocks change often—confirm unblocking on the provider site before you pay.
    What is the most private VPN?
    Proton VPN scores 98/100 for privacy—the highest in our index—thanks to Swiss jurisdiction, open-source apps, and independent Securitum audits. Mullvad (95/100) is best for anonymous signup with cash or crypto and no email required.
    Is there a good free VPN in 2026?
    Proton VPN's free tier is the strongest audited option with no data cap and a verified no-logs policy. Windscribe (10 GB/mo), hide.me (10 GB/mo), and TunnelBear (2 GB/mo) are legitimate alternatives. Avoid unknown free VPNs that may sell your data.
    Which VPN works in China?
    ExpressVPN and VyprVPN (Chameleon protocol) score highest for restrictive-network use in our editorial index. Obfuscated protocols matter more than raw speed. VPN rules in China change—verify current regulations and install apps before you travel.
    Which VPN supports unlimited device connections?
    Surfshark, Private Internet Access, Windscribe, and IPVanish offer unlimited simultaneous connections. Surfshark at $2.19/mo combines unlimited devices with a 90/100 overall editorial score—the best value in that group.
    What is the cheapest VPN worth using?
    Ivacy lists from $1.16/mo but lacks an independent audit. Surfshark ($2.19/mo) offers better transparency with unlimited devices at a low price. Always check the total multi-year cost, not the headline monthly rate.
    How does a VPN protect your privacy?
    A VPN encrypts traffic between your device and a remote server, hiding your IP from sites and your ISP. A verified no-logs policy plus independent audits (Deloitte, KPMG, Cure53) matter more than marketing claims. Use a kill switch to block leaks if the tunnel drops.
    How does this site score VPNs?
    We use an eight-category editorial score (0–100) from public audits, jurisdiction, features, and published pricing: Privacy 25%, Speed 20%, Security 15%, Streaming 10%, Value 10%, Ethics 10%, Apps 5%, Connections 5%. No paid placement. See data sources.
    What is the best VPN for students in 2026?
    Surfshark ($2.19/mo, unlimited devices) suits shared flats and multiple gadgets. Proton VPN's free tier works for tight budgets if you need audited privacy. Windscribe's build-a-plan lets students pay for only the countries they need.
    Which VPN has the best WireGuard support?
    NordVPN (NordLynx), ExpressVPN (Lightway), Surfshark, Proton VPN, and Mullvad all ship mature WireGuard implementations. Our speed category scores protocol support and public benchmarks—WireGuard generally beats legacy OpenVPN on latency and throughput.
    What is the best audited no-logs VPN?
    Proton VPN, Mullvad, NordVPN (Deloitte), and ExpressVPN (KPMG) have published independent no-logs audits. Proton leads our privacy score at 98/100; Mullvad is strongest for anonymous payment without an email address.
    Can you use a VPN on a router?
    Yes—NordVPN, ExpressVPN, Surfshark, Proton VPN, and PIA provide router setup guides or firmware options. Router VPNs protect every device on Wi‑Fi, including smart TVs and consoles that lack native apps. Setup is advanced but covers the whole household.
    Is a VPN legal in the UK?
    VPNs are legal to use in the UK for privacy and security. They do not make illegal activity legal. Some streaming services restrict VPN access in their terms of service, which may block your stream but is not a criminal offence for personal use.
