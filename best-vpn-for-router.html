FAQ

Does a router VPN slow down my whole network?

Yes — all traffic passes through one encrypted tunnel, so router CPU becomes the bottleneck. Use WireGuard or Lightway where supported, and pick a router with a capable processor for 500 Mbps+ home lines.

Can I use a VPN on my ISP router?

Most ISP-supplied routers cannot run VPN client software. You typically need a compatible third-party router (Asus, Netgear, GL.iNet) or a VPN-enabled mesh system.

Is router VPN better than per-device apps?

Router VPN protects devices that cannot run apps (Apple TV, PlayStation, printers). Per-device apps offer split tunnelling and are easier to troubleshoot — many users combine both.
