Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Best VPN Match (bestvpnmatch.com) is a static information website. We do not operate user accounts or sell personal data.

What we collect

We use Matomo analytics to understand page views, comparison actions, outbound provider-link clicks, referrers, device/browser type and basic usage patterns. This helps us improve the decision engine and see which comparison journeys are useful.

Matomo runs in consent mode. You can continue with essential-only analytics or allow analytics cookies, which improves session and return-visit accuracy. We do not collect emails on this site. Your browser may also store local preferences such as light/dark theme (localStorage key: vpn-theme) and analytics choice (localStorage key: bestvpnmatch-analytics-cookie-consent).

Third-party links

Outbound links go to VPN provider websites and may be recorded as outbound-link events in Matomo. Those provider sites have their own privacy policies.
