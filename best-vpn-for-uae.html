FAQ

Is VPN use legal in the UAE?

UAE law restricts VPN use for committing crimes or accessing blocked content. Personal privacy use exists in a grey area — consult current UAE regulations and employer policies. This page is not legal advice.

Why do VPNs fail in Dubai?

ISPs use deep packet inspection to detect OpenVPN and standard WireGuard handshakes. Obfuscated protocols (Lightway, Nord Obfuscated, Surfshark Camouflage) disguise traffic as normal HTTPS.

Should I install the VPN before travelling to UAE?

Yes — download and test the app before departure. App stores may be restricted once you arrive, and hotel Wi‑Fi may block VPN download pages.
